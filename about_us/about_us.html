About Us

Welcome to CatDog, a website focused on animal shelters, created by three AITU students. This project is both an educational endeavor and a reflection of our passion for helping animals find their forever homes. CatDog is more than just a platform for pet adoption; it represents creative energy, innovation, and a deep commitment to supporting animals in need.

Join us in making a positive impact as we create a space dedicated to those who value individuality and a creative approach to animal care.

FAQ

How can I adopt a pet?

You can visit our shelter during working hours or check the available pets on our website. Fill out the adoption form, and one of our team members will guide you through the process.

What do I need to bring when adopting a pet?

Please bring a valid ID and proof of residence. It's also a good idea to bring any other household members or pets to meet the animal.

How can I volunteer at the shelter?

We always welcome volunteers! You can apply through our website or visit the shelter to sign up for various tasks such as walking dogs, cleaning, or helping with events.
